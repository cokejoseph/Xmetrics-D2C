# Product

## Register

product

## Users

D2C brand operators in India: founders, ops leads, and fulfillment managers running e-commerce brands on Shopify, Amazon, and other Indian marketplaces. They work during business hours across desktop and mobile, with a moderate-to-high density of tasks: approving orders, monitoring RTO risk, reviewing exceptions, and checking the daily brief.

## Product Purpose

Xmetrics is a D2C intelligence command center for Indian brands. It consolidates orders, payments, fulfillment, exceptions, customer intelligence, and demand forecasting into a single operations hub. The product replaces fragmented spreadsheets and courier dashboards with real-time RTO risk scoring, automated exception detection, and WhatsApp-ready daily briefs.

Success: an ops team with 10 minutes and a phone can get a full picture of the business and take meaningful action before opening a laptop.

## Brand Personality

Precise, fast, trustworthy. Like Linear for operations — every pixel earns its place by making a decision faster or safer.

## Anti-references

- Shopify admin: dated, information-dense to the point of paralysis
- Busy Indian logistics portals (Shiprocket, Delhivery): crowded, inconsistent, low visual trust
- Generic SaaS dashboards with gradient hero-metrics and identical card grids
- AI-generated UIs with bounce easing, gradient text, and emoji-heavy toasts

## Design Principles

1. **Intelligence surfaces at the decision point.** RTO scores, reorder nudges, and exception flags appear where the operator is already making a decision — not in a separate analytics tab.
2. **Speed beats thoroughness.** Dense, scannable tables with inline actions beat wizard flows. One click should complete any task.
3. **Clarity over decoration.** No gradient, glow, or motion unless it communicates something. Empty states teach; they don't entertain.
4. **Indian market context always.** Currency in INR, date formats for the Indian market, channels (Meesho, JioMart, Amazon India, Flipkart) treated as first-class.
5. **Dark mode is not optional.** Operations teams run Xmetrics in warehouse environments and on secondary monitors where dark mode reduces eye strain.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA target (4.5:1 contrast for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
- Full keyboard navigation
- Screen reader support for data tables and interactive actions
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion
- Touch targets ≥ 44px for mobile-first warehouse floor usage
